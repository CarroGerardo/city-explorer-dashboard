Tokyo, Japan

Population: 37 million

Landmarks: Tokyo Tower, Shibuya Crossing, Senso-ji Temple

Travel Tip: Best visited in cherry blossom season (March-April) for stunning views.

New York, USA

Population: 8.4 million

Landmarks: Statue of Liberty, Central Park, Times Square

Travel Tip: Explore on foot or by subway; try street food like hot dogs and pretzels.

Paris, France

Population: 2.1 million

Landmarks: Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, Notre-Dame Cathedral

Travel Tip: Visit in summer for outdoor cafes; don't miss croissants and crepes.
